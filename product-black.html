Why Choose Our Black Tea

Prime Growing Regions

Our black teas come from the renowned tea-producing provinces of Hunan and Guizhou, known for their ideal climate, rich soil, and centuries of tea-making heritage.

Modern & Traditional Methods

We offer both CTC (Crush, Tear, Curl) for quick-brewing tea bags and Orthodox processing for whole-leaf teas, ensuring consistent quality for every application.

Rich, Smooth Flavor

Our black teas are fully fermented to develop their signature malty sweetness, brisk astringency, and deep amber liquor. Perfect for everyday drinking or premium blends.
