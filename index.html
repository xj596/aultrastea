About AultrasTea

Guizhou Aultras Tea Co., Ltd. is a professional tea enterprise integrating tea planting, production, processing and export. We are affiliated to Xiangfeng Tea Group, one of the largest tea enterprises in China, with more than 20 years of experience in tea production and international trade.

We have our own organic tea plantations in Guizhou and Hunan, with a complete production system and strict quality control system. Our products have passed EU organic, US organic, HACCP, ISO and other international certifications, and are exported to more than 70 countries and regions around the world.
