Why Choose Our Green Tea

Premium Origin

Sourced from high-altitude organic tea gardens in Guizhou province, where the clean air and mineral-rich soil create exceptional tea leaves with superior flavor profiles.

Traditional Craft

Expert artisans use traditional processing methods including pan-firing, rolling, and careful drying to preserve the natural antioxidants and distinctive fresh aroma of green tea.

Exceptional Taste

Our green teas offer a perfect balance of sweetness, umami, and refreshing grassy notes. Each variety has its unique character - from mellow Chunmee to bold Gunpowder.

Pro Tips

  • Use fresh, filtered water for the best taste
  • Do not use boiling water - it will make the tea bitter
  • Green tea can be re-steeped 2-3 times, each with slightly longer time
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
